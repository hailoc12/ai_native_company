# Code of Conduct

## Bao cam chung

Du an **AI Native Company — Community Edition** cam ket cung cap moi truong chao don, bao tram va khong phan biet cho tat ca moi nguoi tham gia — bat ke tuoi tac, kieu dang, khuyet tat, dan toc, gioi tinh, ban dang tinh, chi tieu hang hoa, quoc tich, ngoai hinh, chung toc, ton giao, hoac khuynh huong tinh duc.

## Tieu chuan cua chung ta

**Hanh vi duoc khuyen khich:**

- Su dung ngoai lech va ton trong
- Chap nhan nhin nhan toi nhiet liet
- Tap trung vao dieu tot nhat cho cong dong
- The hien cam thong voi nhung nguoi khac

**Hanh vi khong chap nhan:**

- Su dung ngon ngu hoac hinh anh co tinh dich thuc
- Quay roy, comment khong than thien, hoac tan cong ca nhan
- Phat bo lan thong tin ca nhan cua nguoi khac
- Bat ky hanh vi nao co the duoc xem la khong phu hop trong moi truong chuyen nghiep

## Ap dung

Tieu chuan nay ap dung cho tat ca khong gian cua du an: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, va bat ky noi nao du an duoc dai dien.

## Thuc thi

Vi pham co the dan den canh bao, yeu cau sua doi, hoac cam tham gia — tuy muc do nghiem trong.
