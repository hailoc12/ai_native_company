## sample-company — AI Native Solutions (AINS)

Cong ty mau OPC thuc te, van hanh boi 1 nguoi + 10 AI Workers.

**Cau truc chinh:**
```
sample-company/
├── opc-ains/                    ← Cong ty AINS day du
│   ├── 01-growth/               ← Marketing + Sales
│   │   ├── _knowledge/          ← KWSR: Knowledge
│   │   ├── _workflow/           ← KWSR: Workflow
│   │   ├── _skills-agents/      ← KWSR: Skill
│   │   ├── _rules/              ← KWSR: Rule
│   │   ├── ai-workers/          ← AI Worker profiles
│   │   ├── ai_workforce/        ← Installed Claude Skills
│   │   └── ...                  ← SOP folders
│   ├── 02-product/              ← Curriculum
│   ├── 03-delivery/             ← Teaching
│   ├── 04-student-success/      ← Student support
│   └── 05-backoffice/           ← Admin + Finance + HR
├── 01-analysis/                 ← Phan tich chuyen doi
└── 02-design/                   ← Roadmap thiet ke
```

**Dung de:**
- Tham khao cach thiet ke cong ty OPC
- Copy lam mau va sua theo doanh nghiep cua ban
- Hieu KWSR Framework trong thuc te

## sample-second-brain — Second Brain cho doanh nghiep

Cau truc Second Brain theo mo hinh 8 phong ban:

```
sample-second-brain/
├── 00-company/                  ← Company charter, org chart, glossary
├── 01-knowledge-acquisition/    ← Thu thap kien thuc
├── 02-knowledge-processing/     ← Xu ly kien thuc
├── 03-knowledge-curation/       ← Curate kien thuc
├── 04-knowledge-application/    ← Ung dung kien thuc
├── 05-personal-development/     ← Phat trien ca nhan
├── 06-product/                  ← San pham
├── 07-hr/                       ← Nhan su
├── 08-finance/                  ← Tai chinh
└── _ai-workforce/               ← AI Workforce map + build plan
```

**Dung de:**
- Tham khao Second Brain structure cho doanh nghiep
- Copy va dieu chinh theo nganh cua ban
- Xay dung AI Workforce cho Second Brain
