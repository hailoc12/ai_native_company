## Primary Activities

### 1. Inbound Logistics (Thu nhận đầu vào)
**AI Worker**: AI Knowledge Manager
**Hoạt động**:
- Thu thập market intelligence, competitor data
- Capture customer insights (interviews, feedback)
- Curate industry trends và best practices
- Maintain knowledge base (3-tier: raw → structured → AI-ready)

**KPI**:
- Knowledge articles added: 10+/tháng
- Knowledge base accuracy: 95%+
- Time to find information: <2 minutes

### 2. Operations (Vận hành chính)
**AI Workers**: AI Project Manager, AI Technical Consultant
**Hoạt động**:
- Execute consulting projects per SOP
- Deliver training sessions (content prep)
- Produce analysis reports
- Manage project timelines và deliverables

**KPI**:
- Project on-time delivery: 95%+
- Client satisfaction: 4.5+/5.0
- Project margin: 70%+

### 3. Outbound Logistics (Giao hàng)
**AI Workers**: AI Project Manager, AI Finance Manager
**Hoạt động**:
- Package deliverables cho clients
- Create presentation decks, reports
- Invoice generation và payment tracking
- Client handover documentation

**KPI**:
- Deliverable quality score: 4.0+/5.0
- Invoice accuracy: 100%
- Time from completion to delivery: <24h

### 4. Marketing & Sales
**AI Workers**: AI Sales Rep, AI Content Manager, AI Content Writer, AI Social Media
**Hoạt động**:
- Content creation & distribution
- Lead generation & qualification
- Proposal creation & follow-up
- Social media management

**KPI**:
- MQLs generated: 15+/tháng
- SQL conversion rate: 20%+
- Content pieces published: 12+/tháng
- Website traffic: 2,000+ visitors/tháng

### 5. Service (Hậu mãi)
**AI Workers**: AI Quality Reviewer, AI Project Manager
**Hoạt động**:
- Client follow-up sau project
- Handle issues và change requests
- Collect testimonials và referrals
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunities

**KPI**:
- Client retention rate: 80%+
- Referral rate: 30%+
- NPS score: 50+

## Support Activities

### 6. Firm Infrastructure
**AI Workers**: AI HR/Admin + Company GPS
**Hoạt động**: Legal, compliance, planning, financial management, reporting

### 7. Technology Development
**AI Worker**: AI Technical Consultant
**Hoạt động**: Tool evaluation, automation development, AI Worker optimization

### 8. Human Resource Management
**AI Worker**: AI Knowledge Manager (partially)
**Hoạt động**: CEO well-being, schedule management, skill development

### 9. Procurement
**AI Worker**: AI Finance Manager
**Hoạt động**: Tool purchasing, vendor management, cost optimization

## Value Creation Flywheel

```
Content → Authority → Leads → Projects → Case Studies → More Content
   ↑                                                          │
   └──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

1. **Content** attracts audience
2. **Authority** builds trust
3. **Trust** generates leads
4. **Leads** convert to projects
5. **Projects** produce case studies
6. **Case studies** fuel more content
