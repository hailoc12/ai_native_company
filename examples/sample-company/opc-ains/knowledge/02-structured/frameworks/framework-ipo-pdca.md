## Định nghĩa

AINS kết hợp 2 frameworks:
- **IPO (Input → Process → Output)**: Mô hình process cơ bản
- **PDCA (Plan → Do → Check → Act)**: Vòng lặp cải tiến liên tục

Kết hợp thành **IPO-PDCA**: Mọi process đều có IPO structure, và mỗi IPO đều trải qua PDCA cycle.

## Sơ đồ tổng hợp

```
         ┌─── PLAN ───┐
         │             │
         ▼             │
    ┌─────────┐        │
    │  INPUT  │        │
    └────┬────┘        │
         │        ACT──┘
         ▼         ↑
    ┌─────────┐    │
    │ PROCESS │    │
    └────┬────┘    │
         │    CHECK
         ▼
    ┌─────────┐
    │  OUTPUT │
    └─────────┘

    IPO (ngang) × PDCA (dọc) = Cải tiến liên tục
```

## PDCA Cycle cho AINS

### Plan (Lập kế hoạch)
- **Frequency**: Monthly (macro) + Weekly (micro)
- **Who**: CEO + Company GPS
- **Activities**:
  1. Review previous month/week results
  2. Set targets cho next period
  3. Assign tasks to AI Workers
  4. Allocate budget & resources

### Do (Thực thi)
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Who**: AI Workers (supervised by GPS)
- **Activities**:
  1. Execute assigned tasks per SOPs
  2. Generate outputs
  3. Route deliverables through quality gates
  4. Escalate exceptions to CEO

### Check (Kiểm tra)
- **Frequency**: Weekly (metrics) + Monthly (deep review)
- **Who**: CEO + AI Quality Reviewer
- **Activities**:
  1. Compare actual vs. planned metrics
  2. Review AI Worker output quality
  3. Analyze client feedback
  4. Identify gaps và issues

### Act (Hành động / Điều chỉnh)
- **Frequency**: As needed (continuous)
- **Who**: CEO
- **Activities**:
  1. Update SOPs based on findings
  2. Retrain/refine AI Workers
  3. Adjust strategy nếu cần
  4. Document lessons learned

## Ví dụ thực tế: Content Process

### Cycle 1 (Tháng 1)
| Phase | Activity | Result |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Plan** | 4 blog posts, 8 LinkedIn posts | Targets set |
| **Do** | AI writes, CEO reviews | Published 3 blogs, 6 LinkedIn |
| **Check** | Blog avg 200 views, LinkedIn avg 500 impressions | Below target |
| **Act** | Adjust topics, optimize headlines, increase LinkedIn frequency | — |

### Cycle 2 (Tháng 2)
| Phase | Activity | Result |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Plan** | 5 blog posts, 10 LinkedIn posts (adjusted) | Updated targets |
| **Do** | Better headlines, trending topics | Published 5 blogs, 10 LinkedIn |
| **Check** | Blog avg 450 views (+125%), LinkedIn avg 900 impressions (+80%) | Improved! |
| **Act** | Double down on what works, reduce what doesn't | — |

## Measurement Framework

| Level | Metric Type | Example | Frequency |
|-------|-------------|---------|-----------|
| L1 — Output | Count | # posts published | Weekly |
| L2 — Quality | Score | Review score (1-5) | Per output |
| L3 — Outcome | Conversion | Leads from content | Monthly |
| L4 — Impact | Revenue | Revenue attributed | Quarterly |
