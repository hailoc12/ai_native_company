## Service Categories

### Category 1: AI Consulting Services

#### 1.1 AI Readiness Assessment
| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **ID** | SVC-001 |
| **Name** | AI Readiness Assessment |
| **Description** | Đánh giá mức độ sẵn sàng áp dụng AI của doanh nghiệp. Bao gồm: data audit, tech stack review, team capability assessment, AI maturity scoring. |
| **Deliverables** | AI Readiness Report (15-20 trang), scoring matrix, gap analysis, quick-win recommendations |
| **Duration** | 1-2 tuần |
| **Price** | $2,000 - $4,000 |
| **Target client** | SME chưa bắt đầu AI journey |
| **Upsell path** | → AI Strategy Consulting (SVC-002) |

#### 1.2 AI Strategy Consulting

## Pricing Guidelines

### Discount Rules
| Situation | Max Discount | Approval |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| First-time client | 10% | AI Sales Rep tự quyết |
| Referral from existing client | 15% | AI Sales Rep tự quyết |
| Non-profit / Social enterprise | 20% | CEO approval |
| Package deal (2+ services) | 15% | AI Sales Rep tự quyết |
| >20% discount | — | CEO approval REQUIRED |

### Payment Terms (Standard)
- 50% upon contract signing
- 50% upon completion & acceptance
- For retainers: Monthly, due by 5th of each month
- For projects >$8,000: 30-40-30 payment schedule available
