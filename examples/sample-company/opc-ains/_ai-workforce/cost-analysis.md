### Risk Factors (Cost Increase Triggers)
1. Claude/API pricing increase (+10-20% potential)
2. New tool requirements (CRM, automation)
3. Higher API usage as projects scale
4. Compliance/legal costs if regulations change

### Mitigation
- Budget 10% buffer monthly
- Evaluate alternatives quarterly (open-source LLMs, etc.)
- Negotiate annual plans for tools with discounts
