# AI Workforce — Overview

## Tong quan
AINS AI Workforce la "doi ngu nhan su so" gom 10 AI Workers hoat dong duoi su dieu phoi cua Company GPS va CEO. Moi AI Worker la 1 specialist voi personality, SOPs, va tools rieng.

## Model: 1+1+N
- **1** CEO (Human) — Chien luoc, quan he, decision
- **1** Company GPS (AI Chief of Staff) — Dieu phoi, routing, quality
- **N** = 10 AI Workers — Thuc thi tasks

## Department Structure

```
Growth (4 Workers)          Delivery (3 Workers)       Backoffice (3 Workers)
+ AI Sales Rep              + AI Project Manager       + AI Finance Manager
+ AI Content Manager        + AI Technical Consultant  + AI HR/Admin
+ AI Content Writer         + AI Quality Reviewer      + AI Knowledge Manager
+ AI Social Media Manager
```
