## Content Pillars

AINS content strategy xoay quanh 4 pillars, moi pillar giai quyet 1 nhu cau cua target audience:

| Pillar | Theme | Audience Need | Content Mix |

| **Practical AI Tools** | AI tools, templates, SOPs | "Cho toi cong cu thuc te de dung ngay" | Template (40%), Tutorial (30%), Social (30%) |

## Topic Rotation Strategy

### Thang 1-3: Foundation
- AI basics cho SME (What, Why, How)
- Case studies: "Before/After AI adoption"
- OPC concept introduction

### Thang 4-6: Authority Building
- Deep-dive into specific AI applications
- Industry-specific guides (retail, F&B, services)
- Expert interviews (if possible)

### Thang 7-12: Scale
- Advanced AI workflows
- OPC Blueprint series
- Community-driven content (Q&A, challenges)

## Performance Benchmarks

### Content Performance Tiers

| Tier | Blog Traffic | Social Engagement | Email Opens |
|------|-------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | >500 views/post | >5% rate | >35% |
| **Good** | 200-500 views/post | 3-5% rate | 25-35% |
| **Needs Improvement** | 100-200 views/post | 1-3% rate | 15-25% |
| **Underperforming** | <100 views/post | <1% rate | <15% |

### Review Trigger Rules

| Performance | Action |
|------------|--------|
| Excellent | Replicate format/topic, consider similar content |
| Good | Optimize SEO, test different headlines |
| Needs Improvement | Analyze why, adjust topic/format/channel |
| Underperforming | Stop producing similar content, pivot strategy |
