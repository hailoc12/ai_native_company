## Segmentation Strategy

### By Lifecycle Stage

| Segment | Dinh nghia | Email Frequency | Content Focus |
|---------|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **New Subscriber** | < 7 ngay | Onboarding sequence (5 emails) | Welcome, value delivery, introduction |
| **Active** | Mo >= 1 email/tuan | 2 newsletters + nurture | Education, tips, engagement |
| **MQL** | Lead score >= 40 | Nurture sequence (7 emails) | Conversion-focused, case studies |
| **SQL** | Lead score >= 60 | Custom emails | Proposal, consultation booking |
| **Client** | Da ky hop dong | Monthly update | Onboarding, retention, upsell |
| **Cold** | Khong mo email 30 ngay | Re-engagement (1 email) | Reactivate hoac unsubscribe |
| **Churned** | Het hop dong > 30 ngay | Monthly check-in | Re-engagement, new offerings |

## A/B Testing Framework

### What to Test (Priority Order)

| Element | Variants | Sample Size | Decision Metric | Min Duration |
|---------|----------|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Subject line | 3 variants | 500/sub | Open rate | 24h |
| Preview text | 2 variants | 500/sub | Open rate | 24h |
| CTA text | 2 variants | 1000/sub | Click rate | 48h |
| CTA placement | Top vs bottom | 1000/sub | Click rate | 48h |
| Send time | 3 time slots | 1000/sub | Open rate | 48h |
| Email length | Short vs long | 1000/sub | Click rate | 48h |
| From name | Person vs Company | 1000/sub | Open rate | 24h |

### Testing Rules

1. Test 1 element tai 1 thoi diem
2. Minimum 500 subscribers moi variant
3. Do trong it nhat 24h truoc khi chon winner
4. Statistically significant moi quyet dinh (95% confidence)
5. Document moi test result trong archive

## Email Content Guidelines

### Subject Line Rules

- Duoi 50 ky tu
- Khong dung ALL CAPS hoac spam words
- Include number hoac specific benefit khi co the
- Avoid "Free" o dau subject (spam trigger)
- Test 3 variants cho moi campaign

### Body Copy Rules

- Single CTA moi email (1 action duy nhat)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 cau)
- Bullet points cho readability
- Mobile-responsive (60% reads on mobile)
- Alt text cho moi image
- Personalize voi [First name] o minimum
- Include P.S. cho urgency hoac bonus info

### Compliance

- [ ] Unsubscribe link trong moi email
- [ ] Physical address trong footer
- [ ] Sender name nhan dien duoc (AINS hoac [Ten] tai AINS)
- [ ] Khong mua email lists
- [ ] Double opt-in cho new subscribers
- [ ] GDPR/PDPA compliance cho subscriber data
