## LinkedIn Strategy

### Posting Schedule

| Ngay | Loai post | Format | Muc dich |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|
| Monday | Thought leadership | Text + image | Brand authority |
| Wednesday | Practical tip | Carousel or text | Value delivery |
| Friday | Week recap + insight | Text | Engagement |

### LinkedIn Post Templates

**Template 1 — Thought Leadership (Text Post)**

```
[HOOK — 2 dong dau, gay chu y]
[Viet giong nhu conversation, dung tu bat ky],
nhung [twist/phat hien]

[3-5 key insights, moi item 1-2 dong]
- Insight 1
- Insight 2
- Insight 3

[CAU HOI DE THAO LUAN — tang engagement]

[Hashtags: 3-5 relevant]
```

**Template 2 — List/Carousel**

```
[HOOK — "X cai gi day duoc [ket qua]"]

Slide 1: Title slide
Slide 2-7: 1 tip per slide (keep text minimal)
Slide 8: Summary + CTA

[Caption: Tom tat + CTA]
[Hashtags: 3-5]
```

**Template 3 — Case Study/Story**

```
[HOOK — "Mot doanh nghiep [loai] vua [ket qua] voi AI"]

[Bối cảnh] — Doanh nghiep [mô ta] dang面对 [van de]

[Giai pháp] — Chung toi [lam gi]

[Ket qua] — [So lieu cu the]

[Bai hoc] — [Takeaway cho doc gia]

[CTA — "Ban muon lam tuong tu?"]

[Hashtags: 3-5]
```

### LinkedIn Engagement Rules

- Respond moi comment trong 4h
- Thank + add value cho moi comment
- Dung lien ket ngoai trong comment (LinkedIn giam reach)
- Them personal note khi connect moi
- Engage voi 5 bai viet cua target audience hang ngay (5 min)

## Engagement Playbook

### Comment Response Templates

| Comment Type | Response Strategy | Template |
|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| Positive | Thank + add value | "Cam on [ten]! [Them 1 tip/insight lien quan]" |
| Question | Answer directly | "[Ten] — [Cau tra loi cu the]. [Follow-up question]" |
| Skeptical | Acknowledge + data | "Y kiet hay [ten]. [Data/fact]. [Open-ended]" |
| Negative | Empathy + move to DM | "Cam on ban da chia se. minh gui tin nhan de ho tro chi tiet hon nhe." |
| Spam/Inappropriate | Ignore/Delete | (Khong respond, delete sau 24h neu can) |

### DM Response Templates

| DM Type | Response | Escalation |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Prospect inquiry | Qualify → handoff Sales AI | Sales AI |
| Client support | Acknowledge → route to support | Delivery AI |
| Partnership | Thank → schedule call | Founder |
| Feedback | Thank → capture to knowledge | Analytics AI |

## Best Practices

### Do
- Lead voi value, khong ban hang
- Use data va so lieu cu the
- Ask questions de tang engagement
- Share behind-the-scenes (authentic)
- Post consistently theo schedule
- Test new formats regularly (20% thoi gian)

### Don't
- Post content khong qua review
- Ignore comments/DMs
- Use clickbait headlines
- Post qua nhieu lien ket ngoai
- Copy competitors
- Engage trong tranh cai (political, religious)
