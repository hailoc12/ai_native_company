# Growth KPI Definitions & Targets — AI Native Solutions (AINS)

## 1. Marketing KPIs

### 1.1 Content Output
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Content pieces published | Tổng số bài viết, post, video script được publish | ≥20/tháng | Content count trong CMS | Weekly |
| Blog posts | Bài blog dài (>800 words) | ≥4/tháng | CMS | Weekly |
| Social media posts | Posts trên LinkedIn, Facebook | ≥12/tháng | Platform analytics | Weekly |
| Email newsletters | Newsletter gửi đi | ≥2/tháng | Email platform | Monthly |

### 1.2 Traffic & Engagement
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Organic traffic | Unique visitors từ search engines | +10% MoM | Google Analytics | Monthly |
| Social engagement rate | (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions × 100 | ≥3% | Platform analytics | Weekly |
| Email open rate | Emails opened / Emails delivered × 100 | ≥25% | Email platform | Per campaign |
| Email click rate | Clicks / Emails delivered × 100 | ≥5% | Email platform | Per campaign |
| Bounce rate | % visitors rời trang không tương tác | <60% | Google Analytics | Monthly |

### 1.3 SEO
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Keyword rankings | Top 10 keywords ranking trên Google | ≥15 keywords | Ahrefs/SEMrush | Monthly |
| Domain Authority | DA score | ≥30 | Moz | Monthly |
| Backlinks acquired | New backlinks từ quality domains | ≥5/tháng | Ahrefs | Monthly |

## 2. Sales KPIs

### 2.1 Lead Metrics
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| New leads | Contacts mới vào pipeline | 50-100/tháng | CRM | Weekly |
| Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) | Leads đạt scoring threshold | ≥30/tháng | CRM + Scoring | Weekly |
| Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs) | MQLs được Sales AI qualify | ≥15/tháng | CRM | Weekly |
| Cost per Lead (CPL) | Tổng marketing spend / New leads | <$15 | Finance + CRM | Monthly |

### 2.2 Pipeline & Conversion
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Lead-to-MQL conversion | MQLs / New leads × 100 | ≥30% | CRM | Weekly |
| MQL-to-SQL conversion | SQLs / MQLs × 100 | ≥50% | CRM | Weekly |
| SQL-to-Opportunity conversion | Opportunities / SQLs × 100 | ≥40% | CRM | Weekly |
| Overall conversion rate | Closed deals / New leads × 100 | 5-10% | CRM | Monthly |
| Proposal win rate | Closed won / Proposals sent × 100 | ≥30% | CRM | Monthly |

### 2.3 Revenue
| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |
|-----|-----------|--------|----------|----------|
| Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) | Doanh thu định kỳ hàng tháng | $2,000-5,000 | Accounting | Monthly |
| Total monthly revenue | Tổng doanh thu (project + recurring) | $3,000-8,000 | Accounting | Monthly |
| Average deal size | Revenue / Closed deals | $1,000-3,000 | CRM | Monthly |
| Sales cycle length | Trung bình số ngày từ lead → close | <30 ngày | CRM | Monthly |

| KPI | Định nghĩa | Target | Đo lường | Tần suất |

## 5. Scoring Models

### 5.1 Lead Scoring (0-100)
| Tiêu chí | Điểm | Ghi chú |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Company size (11-50 employees) | +15 | ICP match |
| Industry (Tech, SaaS, E-commerce) | +20 | ICP match |
| Job title (C-level, VP, Director) | +20 | Decision maker |
| Website visit (>3 pages) | +10 | Engagement |
| Downloaded content | +10 | Intent |
| Opened >3 emails | +5 | Engagement |
| Requested demo/consultation | +20 | High intent |
| **MQL Threshold** | **≥40** | Auto-qualify |
| **SQL Threshold** | **≥60** | Sales-ready |

### 5.2 Client Health Score (0-10)
| Tiêu chí | Trọng số |
|----------|----------|
| Revenue (vs. target) | 25% |
| Engagement (meetings, emails) | 25% |
| Satisfaction (CSAT) | 25% |
| Renewal likelihood | 25% |
