## Brand Rules (Quick Reference)

### Voice & Tone

| Context | Voice |
|---------|-------|
| Blog posts | Professional, educational, bilingual VN-EN |
| Social media | Approachable, concise, Vietnamese primary |
| Proposals | Formal, structured, English preferred |
| Email | Warm, professional, bilingual |

### Prohibited Content

- No misleading claims about AI capabilities
- No competitor bashing
- No unapproved pricing information
- No client data in public content
- No political or controversial topics

## Constraints

- Founder time cap: 10h/week max on Growth
- Monthly budget: $600-1,000 (ads + tools)
- All proposals > $3,000 require CEO review
- No outbound cold calling (AI-only channels)
- Client data access: AI workers see only assigned client data
