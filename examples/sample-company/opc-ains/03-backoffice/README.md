# 03 - Backoffice Department

> Phòng Backoffice — AI Native Solutions (AINS) — One Person Company (OPC)

## Tong quan

Phong Backoffice la bo may van hanh chinh cua AINS, bao gom 4 linh luc chinh:
- **Finance** — Ke toan, ngan sach, hoa don, bao cao tai chinh
- **HR** — Quan ly AI workforce, danh gia hieu suat
- **Admin** — Quan ly tai lieu, mua sam, hop dong
- **Legal** — Tuan thu phap luat, bao mat

## AI Workers (3)

| AI Worker | Role | Xu ly chinh |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Finance AI | Chief Financial Officer | Ke toan, ngan sach, hoa don, bao cao tai chinh |
| HR AI | HR Manager | Quan ly AI workforce, danh gia hieu suat, daotao |
| Admin AI | Office Manager | Tai lieu, mua sam, hop dong, backup |
