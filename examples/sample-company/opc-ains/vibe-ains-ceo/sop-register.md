## SOP Lifecycle

### Creation Process
1. **Draft**: AI Worker creates draft based on template
2. **Review**: CEO reviews + provides feedback
3. **Active**: Published and enforced
4. **Update**: Reviewed monthly/quarterly, updated as needed

### Update Frequency
| Type | Frequency | Trigger |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Routine review | Quarterly | Scheduled |
| Incident-driven | As needed | After quality issue |
| Process improvement | Monthly | After PDCA cycle |
| New tool/method | As needed | When new tools adopted |
