## 4. Strategic SWOT

### Strengths
- Unique OPC + AI workforce model (first in Vietnam)
- Extremely low overhead → high margins
- Speed of delivery unmatched by competitors
- Practical, ROI-focused approach resonates with SMEs

### Weaknesses
- Brand recognition con thap
- CEO single point of failure
- AI Workers chua deploy du 10/10
- Content volume chua dat target
- CRM / pipeline tracking con thieu

### Opportunities
- Vietnam AI market tang 34% YoY
- Government AI push (incentives)
- "AI consulting cho SME" gap chua ai fill
- Referral engine bat dau hoat dong
- Content moat xay dung nhanh

### Threats
- Large players (FPT, VNG) co the xuong SME segment
- AI regulations co the tang compliance cost
- AI tools pricing tang (Claude, GPT)
- Economic slowdown anh huong SME budget
- "AI fatigue" — clients chan ngan "AI buzz"
