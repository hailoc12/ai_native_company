# 07 — HR Department

> **"Hire people who are already curious. Teach them the system."**

**Layer:** Support (L3)
**Head:** HR Manager
**Team size:** 1

---

## Department IPO

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **INPUT** | Headcount needs, job descriptions, candidates |
| **CONTROL** | Culture fit criteria (curiosity + systems thinking + consistency), Budget |
| **OUTPUT** | Hired and onboarded team members aligned with company culture and knowledge pipeline |
| **MECHANISM** | Recruitment channels, onboarding checklist, performance review framework |

---

## Core Culture Criteria (Hiring)

1. **Curiosity**: Actively seeks out new knowledge. Has a personal system for learning.
2. **Systems Thinking**: Thinks in processes, not one-offs. Can design repeatable workflows.
3. **Consistency**: Shows up daily. Not brilliant but sporadic.
4. **Apply-first Mindset**: Obsessed with applying what they learn, not just collecting.
