# _rules — Knowledge Acquisition Department

## Policies

| Policy | Rule | Source |
|--------|------|--------|
| Inbox clearing | Must clear inbox within 48h — no exceptions | KA charter |
| Quality threshold | Signal-to-noise ≥70% — reject aggressively | Quality standard |
| Max active goals | KA team receives max 3 learning goal priorities per month | PD department |
| Source attribution | Every capture must have source URL/reference | Quality standard |
| Privacy | No capturing of personal/confidential info from others | Privacy policy |

## Decision Authority

| Decision | Who | Constraint |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| Add new permanent source | Head of KA | Needs relevance justification |
| Reject a source type | Head of KA | Must document reason |
| Increase capture volume target | CEO | With resource implications |
| Process an exception (skip curation) | Head of KA | Max 2/month |

## Quality Gates

| Gate | Criteria | Enforcer |
|------|---------|---------|
| Post-Capture | Has source + why-captured note | Capture Specialist self-check |
| Post-Curation | S/N ratio ≥70% | Source Curator |
| Post-Import | Naming convention followed | Head of KA |

## Escalation Path
- Minor quality issue → Source Curator handles
- Systematic quality drop → Head of KA → CEO
- Privacy concern → HR + CEO immediately
