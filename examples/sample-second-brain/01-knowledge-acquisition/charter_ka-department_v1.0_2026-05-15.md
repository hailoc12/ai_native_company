# Department Charter — Knowledge Acquisition

**Phiên bản:** 1.0
**Ngày:** 2026-05-15
**Department:** 01-Knowledge Acquisition
**Reports to:** CEO

---

## Mission

Xây dựng và vận hành hệ thống thu thập tri thức phong phú, đa dạng, chất lượng cao — đảm bảo vault luôn có nguồn thông tin mới, relevant, và sẵn sàng để xử lý.

## Goals (Năm 2026)

| # | Goal | KPI | Target |
|---|------|-----|--------|
| 1 | Build diverse, high-quality capture pipeline | Source Diversity Index | ≥5 types |
| 2 | Maintain signal-rich inbox | Signal-to-Noise Ratio | ≥70% |
| 3 | Never let knowledge debt accumulate | Inbox Cleared Rate | 100% within 48h |
| 4 | Align captures with learning goals | Goal-Alignment Rate | ≥60% |

## Guiding Principles

1. **Quantity enables quality** — Capture broadly, curate aggressively
2. **Speed first** — 5-second rule: capture now, evaluate later
3. **Diversity is a feature** — Don't get stuck in one domain/source
4. **Inbox = temporary** — Nothing stays in inbox longer than 48h
