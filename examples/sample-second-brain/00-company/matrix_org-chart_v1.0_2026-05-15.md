## Role Profiles

### Head of Knowledge Acquisition
- **Reports to:** CEO
- **Key responsibilities:** Xác định nguồn tri thức, build capture pipeline, đảm bảo chất lượng đầu vào
- **KPI:** Volume captured/tháng, Signal-to-noise ratio, Source diversity index

### Head of Knowledge Processing
- **Reports to:** CEO
- **Key responsibilities:** Phân tích sâu nội dung, extract insights có giá trị cao, synthesize cross-domain connections
- **KPI:** Gems extracted/tháng, Insight quality score, Processing turnaround time

### Head of Knowledge Curation
- **Reports to:** CEO
- **Key responsibilities:** Duy trì vault structure, tag taxonomy, link density, vault health score
- **KPI:** Vault searchability score, Tag coverage %, Link-to-note ratio

### Head of Knowledge Application
- **Reports to:** CEO
- **Key responsibilities:** Bridge tri thức với dự án thực, tạo deliverables, transfer knowledge
- **KPI:** Projects using knowledge/tháng, Knowledge utilization rate, Deliverable quality score

### Head of Personal Development
- **Reports to:** CEO
- **Key responsibilities:** Track growth trajectory, facilitate reflections, design learning paths
- **KPI:** Personal Growth Score, Goal completion rate, Skill level progression
