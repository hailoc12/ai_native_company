# 08 — Finance Department

> **"Invest in knowledge first. Everything else is operational overhead."**

**Layer:** Support (L3)
**Head:** Finance Manager
**Team size:** 1

---

## Department IPO

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **INPUT** | Revenue, expenses, budget requests from departments |
| **CONTROL** | Budget limits, financial policies, tax compliance |
| **OUTPUT** | Financial reports, approved budgets, payment processing |
| **MECHANISM** | Accounting software, expense tracking, financial templates |

---

## Budget Philosophy

- **Knowledge tools first**: DEVONthink, Obsidian, Claude API, research tools are non-negotiable
- **Invest in learning**: Training, books, courses treated as capital investment, not expense
- **Measure ROI of knowledge**: Track knowledge → project outcomes → revenue

---

## Key Allocations (Annual)

| Category | Budget % | Rationale |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Knowledge tools & subscriptions | 25% | Core pipeline infrastructure |
| Learning & development | 20% | Compound growth investment |
| People (salaries) | 45% | Primary value creators |
| Operations & overhead | 10% | Minimize — automate aggressively |
