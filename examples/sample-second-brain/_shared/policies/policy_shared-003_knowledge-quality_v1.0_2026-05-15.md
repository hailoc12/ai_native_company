# Policy: Knowledge Quality Standard

**Mã policy:** POLICY-SHARED-003
**Phiên bản:** 1.0
**Ngày:** 2026-05-15
**Owner:** Head of Knowledge Processing

---

## 1. Capture Quality

| Standard | Requirement |
|---------|------------|
| Source required | Every capture must have source URL/reference |
| Why-captured note | 1 sentence minimum explaining relevance |
| Initial tag | At least 1 domain tag |

## 2. Atomic Note Quality

| Standard | Requirement |
|---------|------------|
| Self-contained | Can be understood without reading source |
| Single idea | 1 note = 1 idea only |
| Evidence | At least 1 supporting fact/example |
| Links | At least 2 wikilinks to related notes |

## 3. Gem Quality

Must pass all 3:
- **Rarity**: Score ≥3/5 — not obvious, not common knowledge
- **Value**: Score ≥4/5 — changes thinking or behavior
- **Portability**: Score ≥3/5 — applicable across 2+ domains

## 4. Synthesis Quality

Must contain:
- Emergent insight NOT present in any single source note
- At least 3 bidirectional links to source notes
- At least 1 application example

## 5. Overall Vault Health Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Tag coverage | >90% |
| Link density | ≥3 links/note |
| Orphan rate | <5% |
| Inbox zero | Daily |
