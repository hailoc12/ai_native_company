## Personal Growth Score (PGS) — Formula

```
PGS = (Knowledge_Velocity × 0.30) + 
      (Application_Rate × 0.30) + 
      (Skill_Progress × 0.25) + 
      (Consistency × 0.15)

Thang: 1-10
Target: ≥7
```
